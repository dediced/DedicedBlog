About Us

Beginnings

You have heard the phrase: "We are what we eat". Can the same be said with "we are what we read", or more specifically, "we are what we do."?

The idea started in a restaurant when the founders found it hard to order food, confronted with overwhelming choices and a lack of quality information. This fueled a bigger question: how do we make the right choices? People resort to high quality information from experts when making decisions. In the internet era, overwhelming information often creates noise and blocks the user's ability to make the right choices.

The team shared a common frustration with finding relevant and quality information, but once that information was found, they also wanted to save and retrieve it quickly and easily. Dediced was created to solve this problem. More than a bookmarking platform, Dediced includes a ranking algorithm to help categorize and organize data based on the interest and expertise of each user. With time, the user will find other hidden interests and talents, ultimately discovering what they love doing. What if everyone can develop their full potential by choosing to do what they love? Will this world function more efficiently and happily? This is the premise behind Dediced.
